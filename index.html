Scammell Plate

These plates were made in 1929 by Scammell China Company. This company has been around since the late 1800s, up until the 1950s. 1929 was the 250th anniversary of the founding of Trenton. The first European settlement in what would become Trenton was established by Quakers in 1679, in the region then called the Falls of the Delaware. Many of these plates were made, and can be found for collectors to purchase online on third party seller sites.
